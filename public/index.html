About Me

Originally from Hawaii, I moved out to Seattle to atten the University of Washington where I completed a Bachelors of Science in Chemical Engineering. It was at the UW that I was introduced to programming when I took an Intro to Java course. The difficulty caught me off guard, but I always liked a good challenge and found myself spending more time on these assignments compared to my major courses. Working in my field, I noticed a lot of the problems I was solving were very static. I didn't want to work in a field that had little to no change year after year, so I took it upon myself to make a pivot and persue a career within technology. Which leads me to where I am today, a Full-Stack JavaScript Software Engineer.
